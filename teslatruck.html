TELSA'S BRAND NEW VEHICLE IS A... TRUCK?

Largest in the world

A new product has hit Tesla's website, now avaible are Tesla's all-electric semi truck. There will be two options to choose from, the 300 mile range model being sold for $150,000 or the 500 mile range $180,000. To put into prespective the benefits of the Tesla truck: An entry-level semi-truck could cost upwards of $100,000 with annual diesel cost be another $70,000, and total operating cost totaling around $180,000 per the year. What the Tesla truck is offering, is a one-time purchase pf $180,000 with no annual diesel costs, making this the better looking option. From the looks of things, Tesla does not plan to roll out this vehicle in the coming year, but there are reports that we may see it sometime in 2019.
