PIXEL 2

Google's iPhone

Our review on the Pixel 2.

Overall while keeping the simplicity we noticed earlier with the 2016 Pixel, Google was able to add a “hybrid coating”. This gives a better texture to the phones, giving you a better hold on it. On the backside, the Pixel kept the glass visor from the 2016 model. The camera can be found in the upper left corner. An improvement with the design, is the relocation of the of fingerprint scanner. It is set where your index finger would be while naturally holding the phone. Compared to last year’s design, this placement will allow you to unlock the phone without smudging the camera and the glass visor. Now on to the front side. The Pixel sports relatively big bezels on the top and bottom of the screen, this is to insure that they could house front facing stereo speakers. The speaker size is the same for both regular and XL models, hence why the bezel is smaller on the XL….
