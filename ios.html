iOS 11

Apple Update

What to expect with the iOS11 update?

We will talk about the change in the UI in a moment, first we will talk abot Siri. Siri will be refined with a more natural voice
and will be more intelligent. Siri will be able to translate English into Spanish, Chinese, and more. Siri also syncs user preference across multiple devices, which allows it to anticipate what you want better. Some design changes occured thorughout the OS, apps like Calculator and Phone receieved a new look. The Lock screen and Control Center have been completely redeisgned. Where you are now able to customize the Control Center and add more quick settings options instead of opening the Settings app.

Which phones will get the update and when?

The iOS will be available to the public on September 19, 2017 for iPhone 5s and later. (To see the complete list of devices to recieve the update, check Apple's website.)
